Shoulder Taps

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 10x

Difficulty: Normal

Target Body Parts: Shoulders and arms
Instructions
  1. Stand up straight with your knees and bend your arms and point your hands in your upper back.
  2. Lift up and bend both of your arms straightly.
  3. Repeat the two steps correctly until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
