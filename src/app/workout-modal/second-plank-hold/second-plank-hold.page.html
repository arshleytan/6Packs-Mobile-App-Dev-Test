Plank Hold

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 15 counts

Difficulty: Hard

Target Body Parts: Oblique, core, abdominal and upper abs.
Instructions
  1. Start on all fours with wrists directly under shoulders, toes on the floor.
  2. Then, step one foot back and then the other as you engage abs and straigthen legs.
  3. Press the floor away from you with hands. You should form a straight line from shoulders to heels. Hold for 15 to 30 seconds.
  4. Repeat until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.

Progress: Exercises Done
