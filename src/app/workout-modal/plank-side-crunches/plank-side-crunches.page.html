Plank Side Crunches

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 30x

Difficulty: Hard

Target Body Parts: Upper body and core
Instructions
  1. Start in a low plank position with your body in a straight line, your elbows bent and under your shoulders, and with your feet hip-width apart.
  2. Bring your right knee to your right elbow.
  3. Bring your right knee to your right elbow.
  4. Repeat on the left side.
  5. Repeat again until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
