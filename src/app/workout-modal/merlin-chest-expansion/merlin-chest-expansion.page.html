Chest Expansion

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 20x

Difficulty: Easy

Target Body Parts: Upper back, chest and shoulders
Instructions
  1. Stand up tall with your feet set at hip width.
  2. Raise your straight arms out wide to shoulder height, palms face down.
  3. Then, keeping your arms at shoulder height, bring them in, across your body until they cross over each other.
  4. From here expand your chest and open your arms back out as wide as you can.
  5. Each time you repeat the movement change which arm is on top of the other when they cross over in the middle.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
