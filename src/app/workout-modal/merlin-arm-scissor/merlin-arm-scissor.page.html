Arm Scissors

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 20x

Difficulty: Easy

Target Body Parts: Shoulders and arms
Instructions
  1. Stand up straight with your knees and form cross arm position (your right arm to the left and left arm to the right straightly).
  2. Move both arms opposite to their position simultaneously. Make sure you move both arms straightly.
  3. Repeat the two steps correctly until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
