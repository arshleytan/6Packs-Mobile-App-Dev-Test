Bicep Extension

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 10x

Difficulty: Normal

Target Body Parts: Shoulders and arms
Instructions
  1. Stand up straight with your knees, open your palm, bend and reach your upper back using both arms.
  2. Lift your both arms forward straightly. Your arms back should be face upwards. Just like the exercise demo video.
  3. Bend both of your arms and reach your upper back.
  4. Repeat the three steps correctly until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
