Basic Burpees

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 5x

Difficulty: Normal

Target Body Parts: Arms, legs, chest, hamstring and glutes
Instructions
  1. Lie face down on the ground with your arms extended overhead, hands face down and side by side.
  2. Contracting through the muscles of your upper back, squeeze your shoulder blades together and lift your chest slightly off the floor.
  3. Slowly and in control, maintaining the contraction in your upper back, rotate your arms down towards your butt.
  4. ause briefly and return your arms to the start position, maintaining stability and the contraction in your upper back.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
