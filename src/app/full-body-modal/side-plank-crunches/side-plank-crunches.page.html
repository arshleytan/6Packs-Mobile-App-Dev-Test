Side Plank Crunches

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 10x

Difficulty: Hard

Target Body Parts: Cores, obliques, upper back and shoulder
Instructions
  1. Upward Phase: Press upwards through your arms while maintaining a rigid torso and head aligned with your spine. As your press upwards, extend your left hip to lift your left foot off the floor, keeping the knee extended.
  2. Bring the elbow on your top arm and knee on your top leg in to tap elbow and knee together, then reach both long and repeat the tap working your obliques and entire core with these crunches.
  3. Repeat until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
