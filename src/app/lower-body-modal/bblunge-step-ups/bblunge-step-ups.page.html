Lunge Step Ups

Level: 4

Sets: 4

Repeat: 14x

Difficulty: Hard

Target Body Parts: Quads, hamstrings and glutes
Instructions
  1. Place a box or a step in front of you and stand up straight.
  2. Step onto the box with your left foot and drive your right knee up.
  3. Step down with your right foot and switch legs until the 2 sets is complete.

2 sets per leg. 20 seconds rest per set. 2 minutes rest between exercises. Progress: Next Exercise
