Flutter Kicks

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 10x

Difficulty: Normal

Target Body Parts: Shoulders and arms
Instructions
  1. Lie down on your back and place your hands under your glutes.
  2. Keep your legs straight and raise your heels off the ground so they are roughly three inches off the ground.
  3. Next, simply raise your right foot up several inches and then as you lower it down, raise your left foot up.
  4. Alternate back and forth in a "fluttering" motion until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
