Side Leg Raises

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 40x

Difficulty: Normal

Target Body Parts: Obliques, hips and abdominals
Instructions
  1. Lay on your side and support your upper body with your elbow.
  2. Raise the upper leg so that it is not touching the other leg. Keep the abs tight.
  3. Slowly raise your leg 45 degrees while contracting the abdominal muscles.
  4. Hold for 2 seconds, then slowly return to starting position.
  5. Repeat for the other side until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
