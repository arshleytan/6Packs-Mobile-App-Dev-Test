Knee Rolls

Level: 1

Sets: 3

Repeat: 10x

Difficulty: Normal

Target Body Parts: Lower back, legs and abdominals.
Instructions
  1. Lie on your back. Place a small flat cushion or book under your head. Keep your knees bent and together.
  2. Keep your upper body relaxed and your chin gently tucked in.
  3. Roll your knees to one side, followed by your pelvis, keeping both shoulders on the floor.
  4. Hold the stretch for one deep breath and return to the starting position.
  5. Repeat the steps correctly until the set is complete.

1-2 minutes rest between sets.
