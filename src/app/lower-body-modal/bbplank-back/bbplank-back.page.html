Plank Back Kicks

Level: 4

Sets: 4

Repeat: 14x

Difficulty: Hard

Target Body Parts: Glutes and hamstrings
Instructions
  1. Lay down so that only your elbows and feet are touching the ground.
  2. With your body in a straight line (Plank).
  3. Kick your right leg upwards as high as you can.
  4. Bring it back down and then kick upwards your left leg and switch leg after 2 sets.

2 sets per leg. 20 seconds rest per set. 2 minutes rest between exercises. Progress: Next Exercise
