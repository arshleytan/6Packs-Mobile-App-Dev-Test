Spinach Nutrition: 23 calories, 0.3g fat, 3.8g carbs, 2.9g protein

In just one cup of boiled spinach, you can get 5 grams of protein.This green vegetable is also loaded with vitamins and minerals. It is an excellent source of vitamins A, K and E in addition to Calcium. Moreover,it is rich in iron and magnesium, two great minerals that are essential for muscle development.The leafy vegetables dark color is a sign of the phytochemicals it contains, which, according to some studies, helps to speed up the body is conversion of protein into muscle mass.
