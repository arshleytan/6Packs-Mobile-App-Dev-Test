Soybeans Nutrition: 446 calories, 20g fat, 30g carbs, 36g protein

Soybeans are among the best vegetables in terms of protein content. One cooked cup of soybeans contains 28 grams of protein. Which is equivalent to the protein content in a 150 grams of chicken. While some people claim that the protein contained in them are not as high quality as other meat-sourced proteins, soybeans remain an excellent choice if you are looking for a vegan or vegetarian diet.
